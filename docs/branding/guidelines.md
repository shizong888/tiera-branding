# Tiera Brand Guidelines  
**Secure. Intelligent. Modern.**

---

## 1. Brand Essence

### Brand Archetype
**The Sentinel / The Strategist**

Tiera is calm, watchful, and precise. Always on. Never loud.

### Core Attributes
- Trustworthy without being boring  
- Technical but approachable  
- Protective, not aggressive  
- Modern, not corporate  

### Emotional Goal
Users should feel:
- Safe  
- In control  
- Confident they chose a premium, intelligent VPN  

---

## 2. Logo System

### Primary Logo
- Stylized owl symbol inside a rounded-square app icon  
- Symmetrical, geometric, shield-like construction  

The owl represents:
- Vigilance  
- Intelligence  
- Night-time protection  
- Silent operation  

### Logo Characteristics
- Flat, vector-based  
- Hard edges balanced with soft curves  
- Strong vertical symmetry  
- Highly legible at small sizes (app icon first)  

### Clear Space
- Minimum clear space equals the height of the owl’s beak  
- No text, icons, or UI elements may enter this area  

### Minimum Size
- App icon: 48×48 px minimum  
- Web / UI icon: 24×24 px minimum  

### Do Not
- Do not add drop shadows  
- Do not skew, rotate, or distort  
- Do not recolor outside the approved palette  
- Do not add extra outlines or effects  

---

## 3. Color System

Tiera’s palette is **cool, secure, and modern**, with a restrained warm accent for intelligence and focus.

### Primary Colors

**Midnight Navy (Anchor Color)**  
- Hex: `#1B1E4B`  
- Usage:
  - Logo outlines  
  - Primary text on light backgrounds  
  - Navigation bars  
  - Security and system states  

**Core Blue**  
- Hex: `#2F55D4`  
- Usage:
  - Primary CTAs  
  - Active states  
  - Brand highlights  
  - Key interaction points  

### Secondary Colors

**Aqua Teal**  
- Hex: `#6FE3DA`  
- Usage:
  - Backgrounds  
  - App surfaces  
  - Large UI sections  
  - Gradient bases  

**Sky Blue**  
- Hex: `#4FA3E3`  
- Usage:
  - Secondary buttons  
  - Charts and graphs  
  - Connection indicators  

### Accent Color

**Signal Gold**  
- Hex: `#F2D04F`  
- Usage:
  - Status indicators  
  - “Connected” or success states  
  - Focus rings  
  - Intelligence cues (eyes, emphasis details)  

Signal Gold should be used sparingly. It communicates importance, not decoration.

---

## 4. Gradient System

Gradients are subtle, modern, and calm.

### Primary Gradient
- Aqua Teal → Sky Blue  
- Used for:
  - App icon background  
  - Hero sections  
  - Large surface areas  

### Secondary Gradient
- Sky Blue → Core Blue  
- Used for:
  - Active connection states  
  - Progress and loading indicators  

Rules:
- No radial gradients  
- No harsh contrast stops  
- Always smooth and restrained  

---

## 5. Typography

### Primary Typeface
**IBM Plex Sans**

Rationale:
- Softer and more human than Inter  
- Still technical and precise  
- Excellent readability across platforms  

Usage:
- Headings: SemiBold / Bold  
- Body: Regular  
- UI labels: Medium  

### Optional Display Typeface (Brand Moments)
**Manrope**

Usage:
- Marketing headlines  
- Hero sections  
- Brand campaigns  

Rules:
- Never mix more than two typefaces  
- Avoid decorative or novelty fonts  
- Prioritize clarity and calmness  

---

## 6. Iconography & UI Style

### Icon Style
- Geometric  
- Rounded corners  
- Consistent stroke weight  
- Flat, no shadows  

### UI Principles
- Soft containers  
- Rounded rectangles  
- Clear hierarchy  
- High contrast for accessibility  

### Shapes
- Rounded squares  
- Shield-like silhouettes  
- Symmetrical panels  
- Vertical alignment preferred  

---

## 7. Illustration & Motion

### Illustration Style
- Abstract and geometric  
- Derived from logo shapes and angles  
- No additional mascots or characters  

### Motion Guidelines
- Slow and confident  
- Ease-in-out transitions  
- No bounce or playful motion  
- Motion should feel silent and precise  

---

## 8. Brand Voice & Tone

### Voice
- Calm  
- Confident  
- Intelligent  
- Reassuring  

### Messaging Examples

**Preferred**
- “Your connection is protected.”  
- “Private by design.”  
- “Security that works quietly in the background.”  

**Avoid**
- Fear-based messaging  
- Overly aggressive security language  
- Heavy jargon  

---

## 9. App Icon & Platform Usage

### App Icon
- Owl symbol on gradient background  
- Rounded-square mask  
- No text in icon  

### Dark Mode
- Midnight Navy backgrounds  
- Core Blue highlights  
- Signal Gold only for critical states  

---

## 10. Brand Summary

Tiera is:
- A premium, intelligent VPN  
- Designed for people who value privacy without complexity  
- Confident, modern, and quietly powerful
